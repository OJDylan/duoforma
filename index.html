How to play

Fill every cell with a blue circle or a red triangle.

  • No more than two of the same shape may touch in a row or column — three in a line is not allowed.
  • Each row and column must hold an equal number of circles and triangles (three each).
  • = between two cells means they are the same shape.
  • × between two cells means they are different shapes.

Tap a cell to cycle: empty → circle → triangle. Right-click to go back. Every puzzle has exactly one solution and is solvable with logic alone.
